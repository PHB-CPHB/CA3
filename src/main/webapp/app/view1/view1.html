Welcome to our site.

On our site you have the following option.

  • You can see our Home site, where you can read about what you can do on our site.
  • You can create a user.
  • You can log in and user our services depending on which role you have.

What you can do on the site depending on if you are a user or a admin.

If you are a User.

  • First option: Home - This will lead you to the Home site where you can see what you in general cen do.
  • Second option: Company Info - This will lead you to the Company Info site. Here you can search on companies by choosing an option, country and the give search text
  • Thrid option: Exchange Rate Info - This will lead you to the Exchange Rate Info site. Here you can see the current rates from the national bank and use our currency conveter
  • Fourth option: Logout - This will lead you to the Home site and log you out.

If you are a admin.

  • First option: Home - This will lead you to the Home site where you can see what you in general cen do.
  • Second option: Documentation - This will lead you to the Documentation site. Here you can read the documentation about the site.
  • Thrid option: All users - This will lead you to the All Users site. Here you can see all the current users created and delete them.
  • Fourth option: Logout - This will lead you to the Home site and log you out.
